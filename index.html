Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [19-02-2025]

1. Introduction

Welcome to Video Ctool! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data when you use our app.

2. Data We Collect

Video Ctool does not collect or share personal data. However, we collect and store FFmpeg commands that users choose to save to our server for public sharing and reuse.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected FFmpeg commands to:

  • Enable users to save and access commands
  • Allow other users to view and use saved commands

We do not collect, store, or share videos, personal information, or any other user data.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect the stored FFmpeg commands. All data is transmitted securely using encryption.

5. User Control

Users can choose whether or not to save FFmpeg commands to our server. The app functions without requiring any data submission.

6. Third-Party Services

Video Ctool does not use third-party services for tracking, analytics, or advertising.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on our website.
